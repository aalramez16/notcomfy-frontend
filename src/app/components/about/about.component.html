About the Comics

notcomfy is an attempt to create visual aides for some of my wackier ideas. Some of the comics are meant to be funny. Others are meant to make you wince. Some are meant to show a fun new way to look at something mundane about the world. Either way, I hope that you find something worthwhile in these comics :)
